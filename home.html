Our Services

Domestic Cleaning

TeplarClean provides exceptional deep house cleaning services that rejuvenate your home. Our team uses eco-friendly products to thoroughly sanitize and refresh every corner, ensuring a spotless environment for you to enjoy. Experience the difference with TeplarClean!

Student Accommodation Cleaning

TeplarClean provides efficient cleaning services for student accommodations, ensuring a fresh and tidy living space. Our team covers common areas and individual rooms, using eco-friendly products. Enjoy a clean, welcoming environment, allowing you to focus on your studies and relax.

Commercial Cleaning

TeplarClean provides comprehensive cleaning services for offices, factories, guest houses, retail premises, shopping malls, hotels, and schools. Our experienced team ensures a spotless, welcoming environment while using eco-friendly products to promote health and safety.

Tenancy Cleaning

TeplarClean offers expert tenancy cleaning services for move-in and move-out situations. Our dedicated team ensures every corner is spotless, making your new home welcoming or leaving your old place in pristine condition for the next tenant. We use eco-friendly products to help you secure your deposit easily.

Window Cleaning

TeplarClean offers professional window cleaning services to enhance your view and brighten your space. Our skilled team uses safe, effective techniques to remove dirt and streaks, ensuring crystal-clear windows. Trust us to make your home or office shine effortlessly.

End of Bulding Cleaning

TeplarClean offers in end-of-construction cleaning, transforming your site into a pristine space. Our expert team removes debris, dust, and residues, ensuring a safe and inviting environment ready for occupancy. Let us handle the cleanup so you can enjoy your new space!
